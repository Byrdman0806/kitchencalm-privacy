Delete Your KitchenCalm Account

You can request deletion of your KitchenCalm account and all associated data at any time. This process is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to request deletion

Email us with the subject line "Delete My Account", including the email address you used to sign up.

Email support@kitchen-calm.com

We process all deletion requests within 30 days and send a confirmation email once your account and data have been removed.

What is deleted

  • Your account credentials (Firebase Authentication)
  • Your profile, household, and dietary preferences
  • Your pantry inventory and meal plans
  • Your saved recipes and grocery lists
  • Subscription identifiers linked to your account in our system

What is retained

Some records may be retained for legal, fraud-prevention, or tax-compliance purposes (e.g., transaction history) for up to 7 years, in anonymized or pseudonymized form.

Active subscriptions

Active subscriptions are billed and managed by Apple App Store or Google Play. Deleting your account in KitchenCalm does not automatically cancel your subscription. To cancel:

  • iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → KitchenCalm → Cancel Subscription
  • Android: Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → KitchenCalm → Cancel subscription
