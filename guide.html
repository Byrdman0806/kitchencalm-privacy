📚 User Guide

Get the most out of KitchenCalm

From your first meal plan to power-user tips — everything you need to make KitchenCalm work for your kitchen.

Getting Started

Up and running in 5 minutes

Four quick steps to your first AI-generated meal plan.

1

Set up your household

The first time you open KitchenCalm, you'll create your household. This powers every personalized recommendation the AI makes.

  • Add a profile for everyone you cook for — even if it's just you
  • Set allergies per person so nothing unsafe gets recommended
  • Pick dietary goals: vegetarian, gluten-free, low-carb, keto, dairy-free, and more
  • Note dislikes — the AI will steer clear of foods someone hates
Pro tip: Add growing kids as separate profiles even if their preferences match yours. It unlocks better meal variety from the AI.
2

Stock your pantry

KitchenCalm's superpower is suggesting meals around what you already have. The more accurate your pantry, the better the suggestions.

  • Tap the Pantry tab in the bottom nav
  • Add items as you buy them — name, quantity, and expiration date
  • KitchenCalm prioritizes items expiring soon so you waste less
Pro tip: You don't need to add every spice. Focus on proteins, fresh produce, dairy, and anything with an expiration date.
3

Generate your first meal plan

This is the moment KitchenCalm clicks.

  • Tap the Meal Plan tab
  • Hit the "AI Week Plan" button
  • Choose your start date (today or this Sunday)
  • Wait about a minute — you'll see all 7 days fill with meals tailored to your household
4

Use your plan day-to-day

Tap any meal card to see the full recipe — nutrition info, complete ingredient list, and step-by-step cooking instructions. KitchenCalm also builds your grocery list automatically, with only the items you don't already have.

Don't like a meal? Tap it, hit Swap, and either pick a saved favorite or generate a fresh AI suggestion.

Power Features

Make KitchenCalm work for you

Beyond the basics — features that turn KitchenCalm into your kitchen's command center.

🤖

AI Meal Suggestions

Need a single meal idea right now? The Suggestions tab gives you fresh AI ideas based on your pantry — perfect for "what should I make tonight?"

🍔

Dine-Out Recommendations

On the Suggestions tab, switch to dine-out mode. KitchenCalm finds nearby restaurants that match your dietary preferences and household allergens.

💰

Budget-Aware Planning

Set a weekly grocery budget in Settings. KitchenCalm plans meals that stay within your budget and tracks your running totals.

🔖

Save Your Favorites

Loved a meal the AI generated? Save it. Your favorites are always available when you swap a meal so you can re-use them anytime.

⚠

Allergen Filtering

Every AI suggestion is screened against your household's allergens. We strongly recommend double-checking ingredients for severe allergies.

🎨

Four Themes

Light, Dark, Pastel, or our signature Glass aesthetic. Change anytime in Settings → Appearance.

FAQ

Quick answers

The most common questions we hear from new users.

Does KitchenCalm work offline?

The app itself works offline — browsing your pantry, viewing saved meal plans — but generating new AI plans, suggestions, or dine-out recommendations requires an internet connection.

Can I plan further than 3 weeks ahead?

Premium users can AI-generate plans for up to 3 weeks in the future. Manual meal entry beyond that is always allowed.

How accurate are the allergen filters?

KitchenCalm screens every AI suggestion against the allergens you list. We strongly recommend double-checking ingredient lists for severe allergies — AI isn't infallible, and we want you safe.

How do I cancel my subscription?

On iOS: open your device Settings → tap your Apple ID → Subscriptions → KitchenCalm → Cancel. If you cancel during the free trial, you won't be charged.

My pantry got out of sync between devices.

Sign out and back in to refresh from cloud sync. If items still look off, contact us at support@kitchen-calm.com.
